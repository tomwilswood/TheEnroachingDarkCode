The Log of Andrea Serrenson.

Entry #001

13th Span DE31

This is Andrea Serrenson reporting from the beginnings of Project Alfheim. We have established an initial group of experts to look into this issue and attempt to provide ideas, at the very least, for counter measures to be put in place. As to what the issue itself is, well. That seems somewhat obvious. Just look up. The stars are going out.

Entry #028

4th Span DE32

Andrea Serrenson reporting. The project is well underway now, but unfortunately, the darkness is spreading. Fast. Members of the team are beginning to lose hope. Some have even started to vacate their posts. They fear the darkness will overtake all before we have any real chance to do anything to stop it. And I fear they may be right.

Entry #103

16th Span DE32

This is Andrea. I'm the last one left now. Everyone else has given up. The darkness covers nearly everything. All attempts to slow it down have proven fruitless, in the end. It just keeps coming. We have, one final attempt now. Our final creation of our small team of experts. Though, as I said. They've all left now. I had to finish it off myself. They all cursed me for a fool. Said I should spend my final moments better than uselessly flailing against something so great I could never hope to stop it. And they may be right. I don't deny them that. But, you see, I have ever been a scientist. And I have one, final theory. That when all things seem the darkest. That is when light matters most.
